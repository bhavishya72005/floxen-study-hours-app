The Problem with YouTube as a Study Tool

YouTube is the world's largest classroom. Any concept, formula, or topic — you can find a creator explaining it clearly. Indian students especially have access to some of the best exam prep channels: Unacademy, Physics Wallah, Dear Sir, and countless others.

But here's the trap: YouTube's recommendation algorithm is designed to keep you watching. You open a lecture on calculus, and suddenly the sidebar suggests a 10-minute Shorts compilation, a trending music video, or a meme compilation. Before you know it, 2 hours have passed, and you've watched everything except the lecture you came for.

Floxen Study Hours was built to solve exactly this problem. It lets you watch YouTube like a focused student — not like a casual viewer.

How Floxen Works: The Core Features

1. Clean, Distraction-Free Video Player

When you paste a YouTube lecture link into Floxen, it strips away everything except the video itself — no comment section, no recommended videos, no sidebar suggestions. The interface is minimal and focused.

  • Full-screen mode — escapes the sidebar and feed entirely
  • Custom timer overlay — bar timer or digital timer, your choice
  • Activity log — tracks every video you watched, date-wise
  • Built-in AI chatbot — clears doubts without switching tabs

💡 Pro Tip: The dashboard also includes a notepad with formatting tools — H1, H2, normal text, and bullet points. Take notes as you watch without ever switching to Notion or Google Docs.

2. AI Chatbot — Doubts Cleared Instantly

Stuck on a concept while studying? The Floxen chatbot is right there on the dashboard. It answers general academic and study-related questions — keeping you in flow without switching tabs. It answers your doubts, not random conversation.

3. Menu — Notifications, Timer Style & Activity Log

A clean side menu gives you full control over your experience:

  • Email notifications — On/off toggle for study reminders
  • Timer settings — Switch between bar timer and digital timer
  • Activity log — Full date-wise watch history with thumbnails, links, and watch time

What gets measured gets managed. When you can see your study hours in real-time, you are more likely to hit your daily goals.

4. Integrated Notepad

The dashboard notepad lets you take structured notes while you study. Format with H1, H2, normal paragraphs, and bullet points — all in one place, no tab switching required.
