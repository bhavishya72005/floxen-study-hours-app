What is Floxen Study Hours?

Floxen Study Hours is a free productivity platform designed for students who use YouTube for learning but often get distracted by recommendations, shorts, and entertainment content. Our goal is simple: help you stay focused, manage your study time, and turn YouTube into a powerful learning tool.

With Floxen Study Hours, you can concentrate only on your study video, set goals, and track your learning sessions without falling into the endless scroll of distractions. Whether you are preparing for exams, learning programming, or following online lectures, Floxen is built to support deep focus and consistent study habits.
